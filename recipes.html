Old Fashioned

  • 2 oz Bourbon
  • 1tsp Maple syrup
  • 2 dashes Angostura bitters

Directions!

1. Stir with ice. Strain into rocks glass over large cube. 1. Garnish with orange twist.

Boulevardier

  • 1.5 oz Bourbon
  • 1 oz Campari
  • 1 oz Sweet vermouth

Directions!

1. Stir with ice. Strain into rocks glass over large cube. 1. Garnish with orange twist.

Black Manhattan

  • 2 oz Rye whiskey
  • 1 oz Amaro
  • 1 dash Angostura bitters
  • 1 dash Orange bitters

Directions!

1. Stir with ice. Strain into chilled coupe. 1. Garnish with orange twist.

MonteNegroni

  • 1 1/3 oz Amaro Montenegro
  • 2/3 oz Gin
  • 2/3 oz Sweet vermouth
  • 2 drops Angostura bitters

Directions!

1. Stir with ice. Strain into rocks glass over large cube. 1. Garnish with orange twist.

Sidecar

  • 2 oz Bourbon
  • 1 oz Cointreau
  • 0.5 oz Lemon juice

Directions!

1. Shake with ice. Double strain into chilled coupe.

Gin & Tonic

  • 2 oz Gin
  • 4 oz Tonic water

Directions!

1. Pour gin and tonic over ice in short glass. 1. Stir gently. 1. Garnish with lime wheel.

Gimlet

  • 2 oz Gin
  • 1 oz Lime juice
  • 0.5 oz Simple syrup

Directions!

1. Shake with ice. 1. Double strain into chilled coupe.

Bennet

  • 2 oz Gin
  • 1 oz Lime juice
  • 0.5 oz Simple syrup
  • 1 dash Angostura bitters

Directions!

1. Shake with ice. 1. Double strain into chilled coupe.
